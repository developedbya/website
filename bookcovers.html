The Secret Admirer


Designed using: Adobe InDesign


"The Secret Admirer" is a sample book cover designed for a mystery genre. This cover aims to intrigue readers and spark their curiosity. The fonts chosen for the back cover were selected to add a personal touch and evoke the mysterious theme of the book, reminiscent of a secret admirer. The photograph featuring a vase of flowers not only complements the title but also hints at the intriguing storyline that may lie within the pages. The use of this image adds depth and symbolism, inviting readers to imagine the secrets that might be hidden among the petals.
