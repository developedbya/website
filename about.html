About Me

I recently graduated from Pace University with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Graphic Design. I enjoy bringing ideas to life through code, creative problem solving, and thoughtful design.

I co-founded MoonFlo, a menstrual tracking web app designed to empower women’s physical and mental health. MoonFlo was presented to an audience of 60+ people at the NYC Design Factory Gala and reached 100+ users. Click here to explore MoonFlo!

As an artist and designer, I showcased my work in Pace University’s Annual Art Student Exhibition in both 2023 and 2025, and designed posters featured across campus.

Looking ahead, I am passionate about using my skills in software development and design to innovate and enhance user experiences. I am eager to explore opportunities that contribute to meaningful projects.
