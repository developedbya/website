Typography

Seraphita Regular


Designed using: Glyphs


Seraphita is a modern typeface with a minimalistic design. Its untraditional approach to serifs sets it apart from conventional fonts. Unlike traditional designs, Seraphita features angled serifs that add a unique dimension to each letterform. These serifs, reminiscent of ancient carvings, give the font a touch of history and craftsmanship. Elegant and versatile, Seraphita is suitable for a wide range of design applications.
